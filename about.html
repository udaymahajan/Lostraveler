About Me

Uday Mahajan

Hi! My name is Uday Mahajan. I am a college student who want to become more than an Engineer. My passion of Traveling is what inspired me to create this Travel webiste. My goal is to provide more and more content to the visitors. This project is to built a site which provides free content to the users. I want to build a community of Travelers where they can interact and tell stories, share their experiences about their journeys to different places. I want that people should be free to upload their photos and share their content in form of blogging. I am currently working on this project so that I can provide a cross platform for Travelers all over the world to interact with each other. This project is currently on trial basis and I am working to provide further functionality. Favorite quote, "All those who wander aren't lost"
